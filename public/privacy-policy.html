Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Immpression. This Privacy Policy describes how IMMPRESSION LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). Immpression is a marketplace that connects artists with art buyers, allowing users to create accounts, upload artwork, browse art, and place orders.

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information from you:

  • User Name: Your display name or account name
  • Email Address: Used for account creation, communication, and order confirmations
  • User ID: An internal account identifier automatically generated for your account

2.2 Content You Provide

  • Uploaded Photos/Videos: Artwork and related images you upload to display in the marketplace
  • Purchase History: Records of orders placed through your account

2.3 Shipping Information

When you place an order, you will provide a shipping address at checkout. This information is collected solely for the purpose of fulfilling your order and is not stored permanently in your user profile.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses (except shipping addresses provided at checkout for order fulfillment)
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Usage analytics (we do not track taps, scrolls, page views, or other behavioral data)
  • Advertising data
  • Diagnostic or crash data
  • Audio recordings
  • Contents of messages or emails
  • Location data
  • Contacts or other device information

3. Payment Information

All payment processing is handled exclusively by Stripe, a third-party payment processor. When you make a purchase:

  • Payment information (credit card numbers, bank account details, billing addresses) is entered directly into Stripe's secure payment interface
  • We never receive, access, or store your payment information
  • Stripe's use of your payment information is governed by their own privacy policy, available at https://stripe.com/privacy
  • During testing phases, payments may be disabled or processed in Stripe's test mode

We only receive confirmation of successful or failed transactions from Stripe, without any sensitive payment details.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: Creating and maintaining your user account
  • Service Functionality: Enabling you to upload artwork, browse the marketplace, and interact with other users
  • Order Processing: Processing and fulfilling your purchases
  • Communication: Sending you order confirmations, updates, and responding to your inquiries
  • Service Improvement: Improving and maintaining the functionality of our Service

We do not:

  • Use your data for tracking or behavioral analysis
  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your information for advertising purposes
  • Use your information for any purpose not directly related to providing our Service

5. Information Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We may share your information with the following types of third-party service providers:

  • Payment Processor: Stripe processes all payments. You provide payment information directly to Stripe, and we do not have access to this information.
  • Cloud Hosting and Storage Providers: We use cloud infrastructure providers to host our application and store data (including user accounts and uploaded artwork). These providers may process your data as necessary to operate the Service.

5.2 What We Do NOT Use

  • We do not use advertising networks or analytics SDKs
  • We do not use tracking pixels or cookies for advertising purposes
  • We do not share data with data brokers or marketing platforms

5.3 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency), or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data transmission occurs over encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure with appropriate safeguards

While we strive to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your data using reasonable and appropriate measures.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the Service. If you request account deletion (see Section 8), we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.
